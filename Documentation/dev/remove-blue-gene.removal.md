**[Breaking]** Removed Blue Gene/L, Blue Gene/P, and Blue Gene/Q support: the per-arch CMake branches, the `bgl` and `bgq` clock implementations under `core/clock/`, the `USE_BGPM` CMake option, the `imp_bgpm` IMPORTED target with its hardcoded `/bgsys/...` path, the `#cmakedefine USE_BGPM` in `core/config.h.in`, and the `#ifdef USE_BGPM` blocks in `core/ross.h` and `core/tw-setup.c`. All Blue Gene platforms are EOL (Mira retired 2019, Sequoia 2020) with no surviving deployments. PowerPC support continues for `ppc64` and `ppc64le` (current Power9/Power10).
